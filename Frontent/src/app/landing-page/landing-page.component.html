About

The DAPHNE (DAta for Neutrons and PHotons NEutrons) initiative is a German national research project that aims to establish a sustainable research infrastructure for data analysis and processing in the field of photon and neutron research. This infrastructure will support scientific research in a variety of areas, including materials science, physics, chemistry, and biology.
